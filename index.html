Frequently asked questions

Is SalonMenu.pk free for customers?

Yes. Browsing salons and sending WhatsApp booking requests is free for customers.

Are salons verified?

Demo salons are curated manually. Full verification workflow is coming soon.

How do bookings work?

Select services, fill the booking form, and send your request to WhatsApp instantly.

How do I list my salon on SalonMenu.pk?

Tap List Your Salon and message us on WhatsApp to get your digital menu live.

Can I compare salon prices?

Yes. Compare service menus, packages, and price levels across salons in your city.

Can I cancel or reschedule my booking?

Contact the salon directly on WhatsApp. In-app rescheduling is coming soon.
